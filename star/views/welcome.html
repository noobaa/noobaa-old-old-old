WHAT IS NOOBAA?

Regular cloud services are good for storing documents.
NooBaa is for all the rest.

Get more space anytime you want using free space on your existing hard drive. With so much free capacity in hand, you can fit all your data in one location. NooBaa is perfect for media files which no other cloud can handle.



Who should use NooBaa?

Anyone who has lots of extreme digital data like pictures and movies, and can't afford spending $100 a month on storage.

Your files can be accesible from anywhere, protected from disk failures, and easily shared with your friends, and it's free!

What can I store on NooBaa?

You can store all your files on NooBaa.

Your files stay private in your account and since it's free you don't need to think if you should store it on the cloud or not.

You don't need to worry about backups - your data is already protected by data redundancy and accesible directly from the cloud.



How does NooBaa work?

It's really simple:
Allocate 100GB on your local hard drive.
Get 100GB of protected, fast, always accessible cloud storage for free.

The more you allocate, the more space you get and better performance.

Under the hood

NooBaa is a computer resources co-sharing social aware solution.

It uses the hard disk space allocated on its members computers' to store encrypted chunks of binary data which composes a very big pool of storage. Trying to access the local allocation is equivalent to trying to read a document from a pile of shredded paper in a waste disposal site.

When users add files to their account, NooBaa stores the file data using the entire pool of storage by encrypting and spreading the data chunks.

When users access a file, NooBaa reconstructs the file and send it to the user over a secure connection. As each file is spread across many locations, it is protected against hardware failure, theft, etc. and the performance is not bounded by a single stream to the Internet.
