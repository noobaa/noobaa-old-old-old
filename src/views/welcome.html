How it works

FREE

Our space plans (100GB, 200GB, 400GB, etc.) are free. What we ask is that you install our client on your device, and allocate unused disk space. The more space you allocate to the client the more you get in the cloud. It is a 1-1 ratio. You can try out the service before you allocate.

PRIVATE

We worry about privacy very much. As such we picked a very strong and well tested encryption for your data (AES 256 bits). Your data will be kept private unless you decide to share it with friends.
